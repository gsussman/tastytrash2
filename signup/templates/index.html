Why TastyTrash

The Problem

NYC has a lot of tasty trash. Every year, we throw away 3.1 million tons of residential trash. That means that every six weeks we throw away an Empire State Buildingâ€™s worth of trash! Trash ends up in landfills, where it slowly decomposes and produces methane, polluting the air, water, and land.

The Solution

The good news is the largest portion of our trash is compostable - 34% - or one million tons (~3 Empire State Buildings), with the next largest portion being recyclables.

The Problem With the Solution

While we recycle 79% of our cardboard, we only compost about 5% of our organic waste. We get it. Composting is hard - you need space, time, and attention. Hot-composting requires a critical mass that heats itself and needs to be turned based on its size and the microorganisms inside of it digesting the organic matter. Try doing all that in your one-bedroom.

The Solution for the Problem with the Solution

TrastyTrash. We love your trash. Just fill up your bucket, schedule a pickup, and we do the rest. No mess, no guilt, no problems.
